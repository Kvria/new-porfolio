Why a butterfly?.....

Usually, butterflies are a representation of deep and powerful presentations of life. Many cultures associate butterflies with souls. Around the world, people view butterflies as representing endurance,hope, change and life.

Well as for me, more or less the same thing but with an element of freedom.

This is where by people are free to express their beliefs, beauty and cultures in different ways without any judgement whatsoever.

This in my opinion is represented by the different patterns and colours on their wings.

Fact:Did you know a butterfly can't fly when it's cold?
